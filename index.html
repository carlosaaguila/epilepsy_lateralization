Calcultor Information:

Purpose: Use the relative proportion of interictal spikes in the left and right temporal lobes to predict the side (left or right) of the seizure onset zone in patients with temporal lobe epilepsy undergoing intracranial EEG.

Instructions for use: Choose five or more randomly chosen one-minute segments of intracranial EEG data in sleep. Count (manually or using an automated spike detector) the number of spikes in the left temporal lobe and in the right temporal lobe. Input these values in the calculator. The units (spikes/minute or spikes/hour, etc.) do not matter in the calculation.
